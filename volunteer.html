Volunteer with Us

At Rainbow Refuge, volunteers play a crucial role in our mission. If you are passionate about making a difference and helping others, we would love to have you on board!

Volunteers can help out by doing anything from showing support and spreading our message to opening their homes to those in need. We are always looking for more people willing to help with our cause in any way they can. Click on the button below to be directed to the contact page and reach out to our volunteer coordinator via your preferred method.
