Donate to Rainbow Refuge

Your donation can make a significant impact on the lives of individuals seeking refuge and support. There are several ways you can contribute:

  • Monetary Donations: You can donate securely through CashApp or GoFundMe.
  • Fundraising Events: Join or organize fundraising events to support our cause.
  • Spread The Word: Simply telling other people about us and spreading our message across social platforms is an easy way to donate a little bit of your time and can have a big impact.

Choose the option that works best for you and help us create a better future for those in need.
